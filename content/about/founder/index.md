## Anand Vijayachandran, Founder

I live by four core values that guide how I lead, build relationships, and create impact in both my personal and professional life.

## Integrity  
- I show up consistently, address concerns directly, and remain present even in difficult situations.  
- I earn trust by listening carefully, honoring commitments, and doing what I say I will do.

## Respect  
- I treat every person with dignity — colleagues, customers, partners, and community members alike.  
- I believe healthy competition and strong collaboration both require mutual respect and professionalism.

## Compassion  
- I seek to understand others’ perspectives before forming conclusions or making decisions.  
- I proactively consider the needs, concerns, and well-being of my family, friends, teammates, and clients.

## [Excellence](values/excellence)  
- I hold myself to high standards and give my best effort in everything I pursue.  
- I bring energy, discipline, and continuous improvement to both work and play — a trait I’ve carried since childhood.

These values shape how I lead, serve, and grow.
